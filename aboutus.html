About Us

We aim to keep alive and expand the money culture and collection all over the world.

Who Are We?

We are a community that values numismatics and banknote collecting. We have been organizing organizations around this idea for years and, as the Babil auction family, we have successfully completed many international auctions. We still carry out cultural and commercial activities in the field of auctioneering.

Our Mission

To ensure that the culture and values in this field continue to live by bringing together various world currencies and old banknotes with the right owners, that is, you, valuable collectors and value investors.

Our Vision

In addition to being a reliable auction platform on a global scale, while bringing you different types of banknotes, we aim to expand our activities every day and carry the values of the past and present more strongly into the future.
