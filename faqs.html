Frequently Asked Questions

What is an auction house?

An auction house is a place where valuable items are sold to the highest bidder. Our auction house specializes in fine art, antiques, and collectibles.

How do I register for an auction?

You can register online through our website or in person at our location. Registration details and requirements are listed under the "Condition & Pricing Policies" page.

Are there any fees involved?

Yes, a buyer's premium is added to the final hammer price. Additional taxes and fees may vary depending on your location.

Can I inspect items before bidding?

Absolutely! We encourage all bidders to inspect items in person during our preview events or request detailed condition reports.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and other secure payment options. Payment instructions will be provided after a successful bid.
