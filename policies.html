Condition Policies

At our auction house, we ensure transparency and trust in all our dealings. Below are our main condition policies:

  • All items are sold "as-is," with their condition explicitly stated in the catalog.
  • We recommend in-person inspection prior to bidding.
  • Condition reports are available upon request for most items.

Pricing Policies

Our pricing policies are designed to be fair and competitive for all bidders:

  • All bids are final and binding once submitted.
  • A buyer's premium is applied to the hammer price (details will be provided during registration).
  • Taxes and additional fees may vary based on the buyer's location.
